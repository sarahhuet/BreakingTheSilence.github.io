BREAKING THE SILENCE
A serious cooperative card game where players experience the impacts of mental health stigma in their own lives, working together to navigate personal struggles, societal misconceptions, and systemic barriers. The focus is on empathy, collaboration, breaking the silence, and fostering understanding, proving that healing is a shared journey.

Bringing awarness on the impacts of stigma

Fosterering a supportive environment

Promoting communication and collaboration.

Mental Health Stigma

Stigma regarding mental health issues is the primary reason that prevents people widespread from getting the help and treatment they need, largely due to fear of discrimination and negative stereotypes. This stigma comes from misunderstanding and misinformation about mental health illnesses, which is supported by cultural beliefs and negative portrayals in media. This game addresses this problem by bringing awareness while remaining a fun cooperative game to play with your friends and family..

Story

In a world where stigma surrounds mental health, a group of ordinary individuals form an alliance to confront these challenges together. Each turn, they face cards representing various impacts of Stigma that tests their resilience and teamwork. Through collaboration, the group share limited support resources to mitigate stress, resolve stigmas, and uplift each other avoiding being overwhelmed.

Aesthetic

Since this game is meant to bring awareness and understanding of Mental Health Stigma to a large audience, I wanted the design of the cards to be illustrated in a way that reflects the barrier it is associated with, but not entirely realistic and remaining accessible.

I took inspiration from illustrations on tarot cards and Dixit, as they tend to prompt more profound reflection. And since I cannot draw, I decided to use AI to obtain that objective.

Technology

The game was entirely designed in Figma, and developped into a paper card game with wooden and acrylic assets.
